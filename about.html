Our legal status

On 11th August 2021 we, The One Heart Wholeness Centre achieved an important milestone by obtaining our official license to operate as a registered NGO, duly registered with the Ethiopian Charity Commission in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. This significant achievement allows us to carry out our charitable activities and further our mission in the region.
