One Heart Wholeness Centre

At One Heart Wholeness Centre we offer a range of therapeutic activities to children living with a variety of profound disabilities ranging from Hydrocephalus to Cerebral Palsy and autism. Our children are aged between 3 years old and 18 years old.

All of the children are non verbal, doubly incontinent and unable to walk. The staff team sets individual aims and objectives for each child which the staff team then work towards during each therapeutic session. Our aim is for the children to be the best that they can be and live their best lives.

Our legal status

On 11th August 2021 we, The One Heart Wholeness Centre achieved an important milestone by obtaining our official license to operate as a registered NGO, duly registered with the Ethiopian Charity Commission in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. This significant achievement allows us to carry out our charitable activities and further our mission in the region.
