Telebirr super app

1. Open your telebirr app/account.

2. Scroll through the options and locate the More button and click it.

3. From the menu options that appear, select Fundraising and click it. You will be presented with a list of organizations.

4. Scroll through the list to find One Heart Wholeness Centre Charitable Organization, our organization. Click on it.

5. Proceed to donate to the organization. Thank you.
